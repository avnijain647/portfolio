Marketing campaigns for India's largest airlines - IndiGo, from my time at W+K Delhi.
Role: Art Direction
Collaborators: Creative direction by George Abraham and Josie Raphael, Copywriting by Palak Kapadia + Nainan Chopra
In the aftermath of Covid-19, people were naturally hesitant to fly again. The campaign is rooted in nostalgia - encouraging and immersing people in the memories of their previous travels, reminding them to 'Fly Again'. In the first phase of the campaign we targeted a travel-enthusiast couple and used their images all over hoardings, newspaper and online ads calling them out to 'fly again'.
In the second phase we opened this to a larger audience by including solo, friends and family travel.
