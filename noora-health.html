Noora Health
PROJECTS
Noora Health is a non-profit that helps improve health outcomes. As a consultant I collaborated with them to design their external + internal communications using geometric shapes from their branding. This translated to animating their logo & contextualizing it in the different ways it's being used. It also included designing & developing a microsite - a glossary, making it accessible to everyone on their team.
Role: Graphic Design, Animation & Web Development
Collaborators: Creative Director - Sanchita Jain, Support - Noora Health Design Team
