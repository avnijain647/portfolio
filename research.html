&frnds has developed a technology platform that helps in an efficient nationwide distribution of FMCG for emerging market countries. To help them understand the indian market, I was part of the team that helped them in conducting primary research and synthesize the findings in an information architecture. The project also included fleshing out the UX/UI of the digital interface leveraging rapid prototyping.
Role: Information design + Research + UX/UI
Collaborators: Quicksand design studio
The primary reserach took place across tier 1, 2 & 3 cities in North India and the last mile consumer in a rural area. Wholesalers and distributors of FMCG brands were identified in these areas and we interacted with them to understand the current ecosystem of their set-up.
We tried to identify how the primary mode of transport and payments changed across these areas including the advantages and limitations that eah of the methods posed.
